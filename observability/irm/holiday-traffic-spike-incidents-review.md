# Holiday Traffic-Spike Incidents Review

Review-only incident plan. These incidents have not been pushed to Grafana IRM.

Template:

- Start time: `{DATE} 10:00 EDT`
- End time: `{DATE} 10:30 EDT`
- Resolution summary: `Traffic spike due to holiday, services OOM killed and restarting. Node and pod auto-scaling requires calibration.`
- Severity: `Sev-2: Significant Business Impact`
- Description: `Holiday traffic spike results in shopping cart errors and order loss`
- Labels: `region:<US|Canada>`, `feature:shopping-cart-checkout`, `client_impact:multiple-clients`, `impact_type:availability`, `service:cart`, `root_cause:Scaling-Defect-Service`
- Region label: use `region:US` for US federal holidays and `region:Canada` for Canadian federal statutory holidays.

Dates use 2025 national US federal holidays and Canadian federal statutory holidays. Times use the requested `EDT` label for consistency with the incident exercise template.
