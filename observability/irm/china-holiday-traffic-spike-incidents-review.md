# China Holiday Traffic-Spike Incidents Review

Review-only incident plan. These incidents have not been pushed to Grafana IRM.

Template:

- Start time: `{DATE} 10:00 EDT`
- End time: `{DATE} 10:30 EDT`
- Resolution summary: `Traffic spike due to holiday, services OOM killed and restarting. Node and pod auto-scaling requires calibration.`
- Severity: `Sev-2: Significant Business Impact`
- Description: `Holiday traffic spike results in shopping cart errors and order loss`
- Labels: `region:China`, `feature:shopping-cart-checkout`, `client_impact:multiple-clients`, `impact_type:availability`, `service:cart`, `root_cause:Scaling-Defect-Service`
- Region label: use `region:China` for every Chinese public holiday incident.

Dates use the official 2025 China public holiday schedule published by the State Council of the People's Republic of China: https://english.www.gov.cn/policies/latestreleases/202411/12/content_WS67331db5c6d0868f4e8ecd92.html. Times use the requested `EDT` label for consistency with the incident exercise template.
